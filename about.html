ABOUT

CREATIVITY

DANOSS Media is a creative media agency with bright vision towards future! Founded in 2016, Netherlands, by team whose paths and passions has always been visual productions in elements of Theater shows, Festivals and visual Arts. Film invites you to a different reality, it invites you to new roams of endless possibilities, picture can say a thousand words therefor film is infinite, all it takes is the perfect shot and we have an eye for it! Our crew consists of creative directors, marketing strategists, camera operators, editors, designers, audio specialists.
Dedicated professional film crew is here to make an original content for you and your audience. Team work is the key, your vision is our starting point! By building or complimenting your identity with our visual experience the outcome will be fitting for your creative doings.
